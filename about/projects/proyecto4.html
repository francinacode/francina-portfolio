MUT4ANTE mentir0s0

date: 2024

BRIEF

A graphic system of visual pieces developed from an original narrative exploring the artistic profile of the Argentine artist Louta.

Insight

Louta’s universe is built on the tension between the everyday and the absurd. His pieces begin with recognizable situations and, almost imperceptibly, drift into the strange. The result is a form of chaos that appears improvised but is in fact carefully choreographed.

Solution

The pieces start from stable visual structures: a typography like Helvetica and desaturated colors that identify Louta’s world. Then, progressively, it incorporates anomalies, reproducing the same shift present in his work — from the familiar to the absurd.

Concept — “Louta tira fruta” (“Louta Throws Fruit”)

In Argentine colloquial speech, “to throw fruit” refers to exaggerating, improvising, or saying things that are nonsensical or out of context. The graphic system translates this logic in a literal and ironic way: a line of meaning that attempts to construct coherence, yet Louta never follows a conventional logic; instead, he “throws fruit” across the compositions.
