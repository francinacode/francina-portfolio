PERSECUTA

date: 2025

BRIEF

Live visuals developed from the given audio piece Dissipatio Humani by Aldo Benítez, responding to its accents, rhythm, and atmosphere, while constructing an original interpretative perspective.

Concept — “THE PERSECUTION”

Savage, contorted, intense, ecstatic. These are some of the key terms that informed the editing and construction of the visuals. The piece seeks to capture the raw intensity of a pursuit within the food chain, tracing the emotional states of the prey at each stage of its escape — from the initial surge of tension to the moment of capture.
